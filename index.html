Hi, I'm Anuj Sharma.

I'm a plant pathologist.

I am an agriculture graduate aspiring to increase crop productivity and ensure food security through development of genetic resistance to crop diseases. I’m a acute learner striving to learn new things. I’m known to be punctual and hardworking. I’m self-motivated and passionate about this industry.
